About

Crimson Sight is a first person shooter created in Unreal Engine 5 with C++. The player must survive by blasting through the endless onslaught of zombies.

Project Info

  • Team Size: 1
  • Time Frame: May 2026 - June 2026

Core Features

• Developed core C++ gameplay systems in Unreal Engine 5, including player health, primary fire, enemy damage handling, and game-state flow.

• Created Widget Blueprints to display the live updated score on the overlay's text and the death screen's GUI.

• Implemented enemy behavior and combat interactions such as zombie movement, damage responses, death logic, and animation-triggered gameplay events.

Zombie Animation Implementation

• The zombie animation starts in running animation and transitions to an attack animation when it is in range of the player
