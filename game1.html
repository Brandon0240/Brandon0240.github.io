About

In this 2D shooter-platformer game, the player survives against a variety of enemy types in endless waves to collect points. With a diverse array of weapons, the player must manage ammunition and strategically utilize an ultimate ability to dominate the robotic invasion.

Ultimate Ability Implementation

• Developed an ultimate ability where killing enemies progresses the progression UI bar.

• When clicking the ability keybind Q, the weapon controller script swaps your weapon to a machine gun and a barrage of homing missiles.

• The duration of keeping these weapons is displayed in the duration UI.

• When the duration is finished, the player loses the weapon and the rockets

Customizable Loot and Stat System

• Each enemy game object has a chance to drop collectable items such as shotgun, rifle ammo, or a coin.

• These game objects use a get method from a dictionary containing enemy stats and each drop chance.
